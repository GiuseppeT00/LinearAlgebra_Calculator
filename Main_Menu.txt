
Hi! In this program you can make a lot of operation with matrix.
This is the main menu, where you can chose the operation.
Input the number of operation that you want to do, then press 'enter'.
0) Exit.
1) Sum of matrix.
2) Product of a matrix with a scalar.
3) Transponse of a matrix.
4) Rows-Cols product of matrix
5) Determinant of a matrix
6) Reverse of a matrix
